Past Experiences

We are shaped by the experiences that we have, sometimes to a drastic degree. Even six years removed, the experience of playing high school football at a 6A program shaped my work ethic and internal drive tremendously.

Throughout university, I took consistent road trips with friends - going across the country to experience the most unique festivals celebrating art and music that we could find. This took us all over Texas, up to Michigan, and through the Sandia Mountains of New Mexico.

Hobbies

My absolute favorite thing to do on a daily basis is create music. Whether it be via piano, electronic sound design, or random recording and samples I have collected over the years, I love all things sound.
